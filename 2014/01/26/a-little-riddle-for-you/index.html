A little riddle for you

Franci 26 Jan 2014 Funny things

Last night I was sitting down and my mind thought up a riddle all by itself. It’s useful like that. See if you can figure it out:

What can see light but has never seen the sun;

What lives in water but cannot swim;

What can hear but cannot speak;

When it moves it cannot be heard.

I’ll tell you the answer in 48 hours (plus or minus a few)!
