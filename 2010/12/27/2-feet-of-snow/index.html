2 Feet of snow!

Franci 27 Dec 2010 NYC

We have had a great big snow storm that started yesterday and continued through the night. We’re loving it big time! We have a snow drift that goes almost to the top of our fence in the back yard, and this morning when I went outside on the side walk I had snow up to my mid thighs. It’s beautiful, soft and fluffy.

Unfortunately both the kids are sick and not up to enjoying it, but Ben, Lillian and a couple of friends have gone sledding now. When they get back I’ll post some photos!
