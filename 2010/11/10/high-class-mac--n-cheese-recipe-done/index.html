High class mac ‘n cheese recipe (done)

Franci 10 Nov 2010 Food Recipes

We recently had an Italian-themed pot-luck lunch at church (we have pot-lucks once a month on the Sunday we have Lord’s Supper) and I wanted to find a recipe that was not just another lasagne. In my online search I came across this recipe, which I thought tasted very yummy indeed. So I’ll share it here. I remembered to take some photos while making it, but forgot to take a photo of the end product, sorry! I seem to detect a common theme here… You’ll just have to make it to see what it looks like finished. :-)

I call this High Class Mac ‘n Cheese.

You boil some macaroni noodles and add yummy things like basil leaves,

sun-dried tomatoes,

(sliced) hot Italian sausages,

and a bunch of other things. Make the white sauce, mix it all together and top with breadcrumbs (I added Parmesan cheese to the breadcrumbs). Pretty easy and yum!
