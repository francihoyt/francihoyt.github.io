‘n Afrikaanse grafskrif:
\nHier lê my vrou Griet,
\nIn die hemel is sy niet,
\nIn die hel, dit weet ek wel.
\ni.p.v.:
\nHier lê my vrou Griet,
\nIn die hemel is sy,
\nNiet in die hel, dit wet ek wel.

\n

Translated something like this:
\nHere lies my wife Griet
\nIn heaven she is not,
\nIn hell, that I know well.
\ninstead of
\nHere lies my wife Griet
\nIn heaven she is
\nNot in hell, that I know well.
\nGrammatically the English doesn’t work quite as well as the Afrikaans, but I suppose you get it.
